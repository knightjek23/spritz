---
name: sandalwood
type: note
aliases: [mysore sandalwood, australian sandalwood, santal]
family: woody
---

Creamy, milky, almost lactic — softer than cedar and warmer than vetiver. Mysore sandalwood (the Indian original) is now mostly extinct in commercial use; Australian sandalwood is the modern stand-in and reads slightly drier. The note that makes a base feel cozy rather than dark.
