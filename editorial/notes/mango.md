---
name: mango
type: note
aliases: [mango accord, alphonso mango, ripe mango]
family: fruity
---

Juicy, tropical, slightly creamy: think the flesh of a ripe Alphonso, not the green underripe kind. Mango has a softer, more buttery sweetness than pineapple and a fattier body than peach. Almost always a synthetic accord in fragrance, often stacked with coconut and frangipani for full beach-vacation effect. A top-to-heart note that fades over a couple of hours. Common in tropical summer scents and body sprays.
