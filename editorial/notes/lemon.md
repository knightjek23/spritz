---
name: lemon
type: note
aliases: [sicilian lemon, lemon zest, lemon peel]
family: citrus
---

Sharp, clean, instantly recognizable. The sourest of the common citrus notes — closer to a fresh-cut lemon peel than to lemonade. Burns off fast in any fragrance, so it usually lives in the first five minutes alongside bergamot or grapefruit.
