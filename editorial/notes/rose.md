---
name: rose
type: note
aliases: [bulgarian rose, turkish rose, rose damascena, rose centifolia, rose absolute]
family: floral
---

The most-used floral in perfumery, and the most varied. Bulgarian rose is bright and fresh; Turkish rose leans honeyed; rose centifolia is jammy and dense. Cheap rose accords smell like a bath product; real rose oil smells like leaning into an actual flower.
