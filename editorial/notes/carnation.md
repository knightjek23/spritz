---
name: carnation
type: note
aliases: [clove carnation, oeillet]
family: floral
---

Spicy, peppery, slightly clove-like. Carnation accords are mostly built around eugenol (the molecule that also dominates clove). Reads vintage — the note that anchors classic 1920s-50s florals and most "powdery feminines" your grandmother wore.
