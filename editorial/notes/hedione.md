---
name: hedione
type: note
aliases: [methyl dihydrojasmonate]
family: floral
---

A synthetic jasmine-adjacent molecule with a clean, transparent, slightly green character. Hedione is one of the most-used molecules in modern perfumery. It lifts and smooths almost any composition, the way iso e super does for woods. Doesn't smell like much on its own; makes everything around it smell better.
