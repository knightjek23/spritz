---
name: ambroxan
type: note
aliases: [ambrox, ambroxide, cetalox]
family: amber
---

A synthetic ambergris stand-in. Salty, radiant, slightly mineral. Gives a fragrance the "lit from within" glow that real ambergris is famous for. The defining base note of post-2010 niche perfumery. Also: the entirety of Juliette Has a Gun Not a Perfume.
