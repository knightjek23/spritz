---
name: lime
type: note
aliases: [key lime, lime zest, lime oil, persian lime]
family: citrus
---

Sharper and more bitter than lemon, with a green, almost soapy edge. Think the rind of a key lime, not the juice. Lime gives a fragrance a cold, slightly fizzy lift in the opening, but it burns off fast: gone inside the first hour. Pairs well with gin-tonic accords, mint, and basil. The classic backbone of any tropical or rum-soaked summer scent.
