---
name: ylang-ylang
type: note
aliases: [ylang ylang, ilang-ilang]
family: floral
---

Heavy, sweet, banana-tinged tropical floral. Reads as creamy and slightly narcotic. Anchors the Comoros and Madagascar floral tradition. Common in white-floral bouquets where it acts as the warm, syrupy base that bridges jasmine and tuberose.
