---
name: cumin
type: note
aliases: [cumin seed, ground cumin]
family: spicy
---

Earthy, warm, slightly sweaty: the smell of an Indian kitchen and, at high concentration, of human skin. Polarizing in perfumery: people either find cumin grounded and animal-warm or genuinely off-putting. Used sparingly to add a real human-skin quality to compositions that would otherwise feel too clean.
