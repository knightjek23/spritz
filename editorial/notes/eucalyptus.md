---
name: eucalyptus
type: note
aliases: [eucalyptus leaf, eucalyptus oil]
family: aromatic
---

Cooling, medicinal, faintly minty. Eucalyptus reads like vapor rub at high concentration; in small doses it adds a clean lift to opening accords. More common in skincare and ambient products than in mainstream perfumery, though some niche houses use it for spa-like effects.
