---
name: orange blossom
type: note
aliases: [neroli, orange flower, fleur d'oranger]
family: floral
---

Honeyed, slightly green, less heavy than jasmine and less sweet than tuberose. Neroli (the steam-distilled version) is brighter and more citrus-tinged; orange blossom absolute is rounder and more honeyed. The default white floral in Mediterranean-leaning fragrances.
