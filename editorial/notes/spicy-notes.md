Spicy notes add warmth and depth to fragrances, often evoking the scent of freshly ground spices like cinnamon, clove, or nutmeg. These notes can create a cozy and inviting atmosphere, making them popular in colder weather or evening wear. They often blend well with other notes, enhancing the overall complexity of a fragrance.
