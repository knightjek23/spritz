---
name: neroli
type: note
aliases: [neroli oil, bitter orange blossom, neroli bigarade]
family: floral
---

The distilled flower of the bitter orange tree: green, slightly metallic, more grown-up than orange blossom. Smells like a sunlit citrus grove with the leaves still on the branch. Cleaner and less indolic than jasmine, more floral than petitgrain. The signature of Tom Ford Neroli Portofino and Acqua di Parma Colonia. Lasts a few hours, then fades into a soft soapy hum.
