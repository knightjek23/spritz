name: milk

Creamy, slightly sweet, comforting in a way that's hard to describe. Milk accords are built from lactones (the same molecule family that gives coconut and peach their creamy quality). Backbone of "skin scent" gourmands and the new wave of milky-vanilla niche fragrances.
