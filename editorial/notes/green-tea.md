name: green tea
type: note
aliases: [matcha, sencha, tea leaf]

---

Cool, slightly grassy, faintly bitter. Green tea accords (built on hedione plus bergamot) read clean and meditative. Anchored a wave of late-90s tea-themed fragrances (Bvlgari Eau Parfumée au Thé Vert, the original tea benchmark) and remain a quiet workhorse note in modern fresh compositions.
