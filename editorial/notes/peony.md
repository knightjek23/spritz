---
name: peony
type: note
aliases: [pink peony, white peony]
family: floral
---

Soft, slightly green, faintly watery: the smell of a garden flower, not a perfume essence. Like lily of the valley, peony yields no natural extract; it's all reconstructed accords. Reads as fresh, young, and feminine. Common in modern designer launches that want a "spring floral" identity without committing to rose or jasmine.
