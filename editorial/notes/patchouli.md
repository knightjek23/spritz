---
name: patchouli
type: note
aliases: [patchouli leaf, indonesian patchouli]
family: woody
---

Earthy, musty, a bit hippie, but only the raw leaf. Modern patchouli accords are cleaned-up fractions that read as dark, soft, and chocolatey. The dominant base note in the entire "chypre" family and the backbone of roughly every amber fragrance made since 1920.
