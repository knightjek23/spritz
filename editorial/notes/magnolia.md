---
name: magnolia
type: note
aliases: [southern magnolia, magnolia grandiflora]
family: floral
---

Lemony, slightly creamy, less heavy than tuberose. Magnolia in fragrance reads bright and youthful, somewhere between a citrus and a white floral. Underused compared to its potential; when it works, it gives compositions a fresh-but-soft quality nothing else does.
