---
name: praline
type: note
aliases: [almond praline, hazelnut praline]
family: gourmand
---

Sweet, nutty, candy-shop. Praline in fragrance is essentially almond + caramel + a hint of vanilla. Defining note of La Vie Est Belle and the wave of praline-led feminine gourmands that followed. Reads warmer than chocolate, sweeter than tonka, less aggressive than full caramel.
