---
name: cedar
type: note
aliases: [cedarwood, virginia cedar, atlas cedar, texas cedar]
family: woody
---

Dry, sharp, pencil-shavings woody. The cleanest of the common wood notes: less smoky than guaiac, less creamy than sandalwood, less sweaty than vetiver. Workhorse base note in modern men's fragrances and a clean structural beam in countless others.
