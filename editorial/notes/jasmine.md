---
name: jasmine
type: note
aliases: [jasmine sambac, jasmine grandiflorum, indian jasmine]
family: floral
---

Heavy, sweet, slightly indolic, meaning, at high concentration, faintly animal. Jasmine sambac is the rounder, sweeter version (think jasmine tea); grandiflorum is greener and more narcotic. The most famous floral in perfumery and a cornerstone of nearly every white-floral fragrance.
