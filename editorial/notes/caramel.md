---
name: caramel
type: note
aliases: [burnt sugar, salted caramel]
family: gourmand
---

Sweet, slightly burnt, dense. The note that turns vanilla gourmands into dessert gourmands. Built from ethyl maltol and similar molecules, the same family that drives Mugler Angel's signature. Easy to overdo; caramel-forward fragrances tip into "edible" territory fast.
