---
name: lily
type: note
aliases: [casablanca lily, white lily, oriental lily, calla lily]
family: floral
---

Heavy, cool, slightly funereal. White lily reads as wedding-and-funeral floral: rich, slightly waxy, the kind of scent that fills a whole room. Less narcotic than tuberose, more austere than gardenia. Often paired with green or aldehydic notes to keep it from going too somber.
