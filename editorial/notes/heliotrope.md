---
name: heliotrope
type: note
aliases: [heliotropin]
family: floral
---

Sweet, powdery, almond-cherry-vanilla. Heliotrope flowers smell faintly of marzipan; the perfumery accord (built around heliotropin) doubles down on the cherry-pie sweetness. Reads vintage and slightly nostalgic, common in old-fashioned powdery florals.
