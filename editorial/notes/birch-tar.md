---
name: birch tar
type: note
aliases: [birch, smoked birch, russian leather]
family: smoky
---

Smoky, leathery, slightly medicinal — the smell of a campfire and an old saddle at once. Birch tar is the foundation of most "leather" accords in classic perfumery (Cuir de Russie, Knize Ten). A few drops add a smoldering, dark edge to compositions; too much and it reads as creosote.
