name: bergamot
type: note
aliases: [italian bergamot, bergamot oil, bergamot peel, calabrian bergamot]
family: citrus
---

The bright, sharp citrus that opens roughly half the cologne ever made. Smell Earl Grey tea: that's bergamot. Lighter and more bitter than orange, greener than lemon. Volatile: shines in the first ten minutes, mostly gone by hour two.
