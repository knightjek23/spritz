---
name: freesia
type: note
aliases: [freesia accord, white freesia]
family: floral
---

Light, slightly peppery, faintly fruity: the friendly floral you smell at a corner flower shop. Less heady than jasmine, less powdery than violet, with a clean, almost watery transparency. Freesia is almost always a synthetic accord (the real flower yields no useful absolute), which is why it reads so consistently fresh and modern. A workhorse heart note in soft, feminine, "clean girl" fragrances.
