---
name: iris
type: note
aliases: [orris, orris root, orris butter, iris pallida]
family: floral
---

Cool, powdery, slightly carrot-like. Iris is technically a root note — the rhizome is aged for years before it can be processed — which is why iris-heavy fragrances are often expensive. Reads as quiet, mature, slightly melancholic. Beloved by people who hate sweet fragrances.
