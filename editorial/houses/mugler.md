Mugler launched fragrance in 1992 with Angel — a scent so structurally weird (patchouli + chocolate + caramel + red berries on a heroic dose of ethyl maltol) that it created an entirely new fragrance category, the gourmand. Angel is one of the few fragrances that genuinely changed perfumery; everything labeled "vanilla gourmand" since traces back to it. The house followed with Alien (2005), Womanity (2010), and Aura (2017), all of which keep the Mugler signature of being unmistakable from across a room. Now under L'Oréal as of 2020. Loud, polarizing, beloved — never neutral.
