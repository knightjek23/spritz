Diptyque opened in 1961 on Boulevard Saint-Germain as a textile and gift shop; the candle line came in 1963, fragrances in 1968. The house has a quietly literary, slightly Parisian-bohemian sensibility: bottles look like apothecary labels, names lean toward botanicals and places (Philosykos, Tam Dao, Do Son). Owned by Manzanita Capital since 2005. The fragrances tend to be soft-spoken and skin-close, not the brand to reach for if you want projection.
