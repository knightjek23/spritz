Aventus is the most-imitated fragrance of the last fifteen years. The original is genuinely good (bright pineapple opening, smoky birch heart, a confident musk-and-ambergris base), though batch variation is a real and well-documented frustration that the brand has never publicly addressed. The cultural footprint is enormous: at least a hundred clones exist across every price point, and "smells like Aventus" became a standalone category. Worth smelling once even if you never buy a bottle, just to understand what everything else is reacting to.
