By the Fireplace is the Replica fragrance that delivers most exactly on its concept. Marie Salamagne built it on a guaiac-wood smoky note with chestnut and vanilla underneath, and the result smells precisely like sitting next to a wood fire while something sweet is being toasted nearby. Performance is solid for a designer, especially in cold weather. Probably the most-recommended winter fragrance of the last decade. Not interesting in the perfumer-experimentation sense, but it does what it sets out to do better than any of its imitators. Worth owning if you live somewhere with a real winter.
