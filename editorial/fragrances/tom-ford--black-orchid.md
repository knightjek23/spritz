Black Orchid was the launch fragrance for Tom Ford's perfume line and remains one of the strangest mainstream releases of the 2000s: a dense, dark, almost gasoline-tinged composition built on truffle, black currant, ylang-ylang, and a thick patchouli-vanilla base. It's deliberately confusing on first wear and was widely panned at launch before slowly becoming a cult favorite. Performance is brutal: easily 12+ hours and projects across rooms. Polarizing in the sharpest sense: people either think it's a masterwork of weirdness or a chemical disaster. Genuinely worth smelling once before deciding.
