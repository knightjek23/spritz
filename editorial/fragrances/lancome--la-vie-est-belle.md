La Vie Est Belle was Lancôme's response to Chanel Coco Mademoiselle and YSL Black Opium: a massive, sweet, gourmand-floral built by a three-perfumer team to sell broadly. Iris and patchouli are the structural notes; praline and tonka are why people remember it. Won enough commercial success to become one of the best-selling feminine fragrances of the entire 2010s. Performance is excellent. Originality is not the selling point. Wears as a confident "I'm a grown-up adult" daily fragrance for a very wide audience.
